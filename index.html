We are a quality-focused, sustainable blackberry farm in the heart of Tasmania. Our berries are grown with care, harvested at peak ripeness, and delivered fresh to you — whether at the farm gate, across Hobart, or Tasmania-wide.

Our Blackberries

We grow Chester Thornless and Cluster Thornless varieties — premium berries with excellent shelf life and consistent quality for farm gate, wholesale, and delivery.

Chester Thornless Blackberry

Late-season, firm fruit with deep colour and excellent handling. Ideal for farm gate, wholesale, and delivery.

Cluster Thornless Blackberry

Cluster fruiting, uniform presentation, strong productivity. Fresh market focused.
